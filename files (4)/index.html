About

A data analyst who also builds the front end.

I'm a final-year Computer Science student at Sikkim Manipal Institute of Technology, currently working as a Data Analyst Intern at CyberPeace Foundation. My focus is extracting real insight from messy data — cleaning it, modeling it, and visualizing it in a way people can actually act on.

Alongside analytics, I build small, polished web interfaces for practice and fun — from responsive card components to full landing page clones — because I believe a good analyst should also understand how the data they analyze eventually reaches a screen.

I care about clean structure, whether that's a well-indexed SQL table or a well-organized stylesheet, and I enjoy the moment a pipeline — or a page — finally clicks into place.
